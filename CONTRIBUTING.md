# Contributing to KBits

## Recommending Articles

Found an article worth reading? Submit it!

### How to Recommend

1. [Create a new issue](https://github.com/koriym/KBits/issues/new?template=recommend-article.md&labels=recommended-url)
2. Add the URL
3. (Optional) Explain why it's worth reading

### What Happens Next

Pochita (AI curator) will:

1. Fetch article metadata
2. Evaluate against curation criteria
3. Translate/summarize if accepted
4. Comment on your issue with decision + reasoning

### Curation Criteria

**Articles worth reading. Ideas worth spreading.**

Accepted articles:
- Leave something after reading
- Deserve broader attention
- Provoke thought or challenge assumptions
- Teach something new
- Have long-term reference value

Not accepted:
- Clickbait or shallow content
- Quickly obsolete news
- Purely promotional material

### Decision Timeline

Pochita checks issues twice daily (morning and evening JST). Expect response within 12-24 hours.

### Questions?

Open a regular issue or contact @koriym directly.

Thank you for helping curate ideas worth spreading!
